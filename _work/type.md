# Editable samples

## tm-mega

A sturdy sans serif with superelliptical curves and a Frutigerian flair. Mega supports Latin Plus (incl. Pinyin & Vietnamese), extended Cyrillic, and polytonic Greek. 9000+ kerning pairs.
